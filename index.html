A little bit about me.

I've made my home in Soap Lake, Washington with my small family. My journey into tech started with a growing curiosity of how websites were made way back in the early 2000's. As I started googling the answers to my questions to figure out how I could also make a webpage I got really into the whole process of webpage creation.

My desire to create wonderful sites and landing pages opened a door to me that can never be shut. I love building and creating, it's a type of freedom that I deeply enjoy. I hope to some day make the leap to work full time as a front end web developer so that I can use my organizational skills and eye for details to write clean elegant code.
